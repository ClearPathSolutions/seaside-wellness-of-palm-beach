## Understanding Mental Health Conditions

Common conditions treated in residential programs include:

- Depression
- Anxiety disorders
- Trauma and PTSD
- Bipolar disorder
- Mood disorders

These conditions are often influenced by both biological and environmental factors. Many individuals also experience substance use alongside mental health challenges.

## Signs You May Need Mental Health Treatment

It is not always easy to recognize when professional help is needed. Many people normalize symptoms or try to push through them.

Some common signs include:

- Persistent sadness or emotional numbness
- High levels of anxiety or constant worry
- Difficulty sleeping or low energy
- Loss of interest in daily life
- Withdrawal from family and relationships
- Trouble focusing or making decisions

If these symptoms begin to impact your daily life, structured treatment can provide the support needed to regain stability.
